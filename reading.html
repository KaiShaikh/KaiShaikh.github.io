Artful Mathematics: The Heritage of M. C. Escher [pdf]
B. de Smit and H. W. Lenstra Jr.
This article examines the mathematical structure to a famous lithograph of Escher: rentententoonstelling.
Epigenetic programming by maternal behavior [pdf]
Weaver, I.C.G. et al.
This relatively old article presents findings regarding "epigenetic programming" of young mice through differential maternal behavior, which modulates the methylation of a key promoter at this early stage of life. This leads to diffences in stress-responses in adult mice, which maintain the methylation state acquired shortly after birth. A fascinating read!
Debunking the Stanford Prison Experiment [pdf]
Thibault Le Texier
This paper examines key issues with the science behind the Stanford Prison Experiment, which is often presented as a "classic" study in psychology despite its many flaws.
Toward Fairness in Face Matching Algorithms [pdf]
Jamal Alasadi, Ahmed Al-Hilli, and Vivek Singh
This paper presents a method of improving both accuracy and fairness of face-matching algorithms through a generative adversarial network [GAN]-based framework.
